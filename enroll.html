How CareChain NFT Works
Please read the following so you understand exactly how your membership credential works.
📱

Your Digital Membership Card

Your membership is stored as a digital credential called an NFT (Non-Fungible Token) in a free app called Xaman. Think of it like an Apple Wallet card — it lives on your phone and you present it at every visit. It cannot be faked, copied, or transferred to anyone else.

⛓️

What is Blockchain?

A blockchain is a permanent, tamper-proof digital record system. Your membership credential is recorded on the XRP Ledger — one of the most established and secure blockchains in the world. This means your membership can be instantly verified by any CareChain-enabled provider. You do not need to understand or own cryptocurrency to use this program.

🔒

Your Privacy is Protected

Your personal health information — your name, medical history, diagnoses, and clinical records — is never stored on the blockchain. The blockchain only records that you hold a valid membership credential. All personal and health information stays securely in your clinic's electronic health records system, exactly as it always has.

🔄

Monthly Renewal Cycle

Your membership is billed monthly. Each month, your membership credential automatically refreshes when your payment is processed. If you cancel or your payment lapses, your membership credential is automatically deactivated at the end of the billing period. You can re-enroll at any time.

🏥

How to Use It at Your Visit

When you arrive for an appointment, simply open the Xaman app on your phone and show your wallet address or QR code to the front desk staff. They will verify your membership in seconds and your tier benefits will be applied to your visit automatically.

📋

Your Health Record Credential (Optional)

As an optional add-on, CareChain can issue you a permanent digital credential that gives you verifiable ownership of your health records. This allows you to carry your complete medical history and share it securely with any provider you choose — with your consent. Your actual health data stays encrypted off-chain. This feature will be available during the pilot program.
