The Problem

Healthcare data is broken.

Patient records are siloed across hundreds of disconnected systems. Data is lost between providers, breached by bad actors, and owned by institutions — not the people it belongs to.

CareChainNFT changes that. By anchoring health records to the XRP Ledger as NFTs, we give patients true ownership, clinics seamless access, and the entire system a foundation of trust.

How It Works

Simple for patients.
Powerful for providers.

01

Records Minted as NFTs

Medical events — visits, prescriptions, lab results — are hashed and minted as verifiable NFTs on the XRP Ledger. Immutable and tamper-proof.

02

Patient-Controlled Access

Only the patient decides who sees their data. Providers request access; patients grant or revoke it — no middlemen, no data brokers.

03

Verified & Compliant

Built with HIPAA compliance at the core, and partnering with verification infrastructure to ensure identity and data integrity at every step.
