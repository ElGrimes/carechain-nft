Let's Get You Set Up

Follow these simple steps to activate your membership and start enjoying your healthcare benefits today.

🥇

Gold Tier Membership

Your membership is ready to be activated

1

Download the Xaman Wallet

Your secure digital wallet for storing your membership NFT

Current

Xaman (formerly XUMM) is a free, secure digital wallet app that stores your CareChain NFT membership credential. Think of it like a digital version of your insurance card — but more secure and always in your pocket.

🍎 Download for iPhone 🤖 Download for Android 💡 Xaman is completely free to download and use. You do not need to purchase any cryptocurrency to set up your wallet.
2

Create Your Wallet Account

Set up your personal secure wallet in Xaman

Upcoming

Open the Xaman app and create a new account. Follow these steps carefully:

  • 1 Open Xaman and tap "Create a new account"
  • 2 Read and accept the terms of service
  • 3 You will be shown a Secret Family Seed — a series of words. Write these down on paper and store them somewhere safe. This is your backup key.
  • 4 Confirm your seed words when prompted
  • 5 Set up your PIN or biometric security
  • 6 Your wallet is now created
⚠️ Important: Never share your seed words with anyone — including CareChain staff. Your seed words are the only way to recover your wallet if you lose your phone. Store them safely offline.
3

Share Your Wallet Address

Give your address to the clinic so we can send your membership NFT

Upcoming

Your wallet address is like your email address for receiving your membership NFT. It is safe to share — it only allows people to send things to you, never to take anything from you.

  • 1 Open Xaman and tap your account at the top of the screen
  • 2 Tap "Copy address" or show the QR code
  • 3 Share your address or QR code with the clinic staff
✅ Your wallet address starts with the letter "r" and is about 34 characters long. Example: rXXXX...XXXX
4

Accept Your Membership NFT

Receive your digital membership credential in Xaman

Upcoming

Once the clinic sends your membership NFT you will receive a notification in Xaman. Follow these steps to accept it:

  • 1 Open Xaman — you will see a pending request notification
  • 2 Tap the notification to open the request
  • 3 Review the details — you will see your membership tier
  • 4 Tap "Accept" and confirm with your PIN
  • 5 Your membership NFT will appear in your wallet
💡 This process takes less than 30 seconds. Your membership is activated the moment you accept the NFT.
5

Use Your Membership at Every Visit

Show your Xaman wallet at check-in to access your benefits

Upcoming

Your CareChain NFT membership is now active. Here's how to use it every time you visit the clinic:

  • 1 Arrive at the clinic and approach the front desk
  • 2 Open Xaman on your phone
  • 3 Show your wallet address or QR code to the staff member
  • 4 Staff will verify your membership instantly on their screen
  • 5 Your tier benefits are automatically applied to your visit
✅ Think of your Xaman wallet like an Apple Wallet card. Open it, show it, and your benefits are confirmed in seconds.
🎉

You're All Set!

Your CareChain NFT membership is active and ready to use. Show your Xaman wallet at every visit to access your benefits.
